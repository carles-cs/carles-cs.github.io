Privacy Policy for QRReader

Effective Date: August 3, 2025

QRReader is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how QRReader handles your information.

1. Data Collection and Storage

QRReader does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information or usage data. All scanned codes and related data remain exclusively on your device. No information is sent to any external server, nor shared with any third party.

2. Device Permissions & Image Processing

QRReader may request access to your device’s camera exclusively for the purpose of scanning QR codes. All image processing required for decoding QR codes is performed entirely on your device. No images or related data are transmitted or shared externally. No other data from your device is accessed, stored, or transmitted.

3. Third-Party Services

QRReader does not utilize any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Changes to This Policy

If QRReader’s privacy practices change, updates will be posted to this page. Please review this policy periodically.
